Boys Hostel



SVPM provides hostel facility separately for boys and girls. The intake capacity for boys is 750 and that of girls is 750.The Instituted provides bed, writing table, chair, fan, tube light with table lamp connection for each inmate. The separate mess is adjoining the hostel building .Hostel rectors co-ordinate and control activities. Students are strictly required to abide by rules of the hostel.
